# Privacy Policy - Match

Last updated: 2026-04-26

This Privacy Policy describes how Match ("we", "us") processes personal data when you use the app.

## 1) Data We Process

We process the following categories of data to provide core app functionality:

- Account data: email address, authentication provider (email/password or Google sign-in), user ID.
- Profile data: username, display name, bio, profile photo URL, selected sports.
- Social and match data: friends list, friend requests, match requests, match responses, match status.
- Messaging data: conversation metadata and message content between friends.
- Notification data: in-app notification entries and notification preferences.
- Technical data: basic service logs generated by Firebase services for reliability and security.

We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

## 2) Why We Process Data

We process data only for app operation and security:

- Create and manage user accounts.
- Enable friend discovery, requests, and profile display.
- Create, update, and manage matches.
- Provide chat between connected friends.
- Send in-app notifications related to social and match events.
- Maintain app security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

## 3) Legal Basis (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, processing is based on:

- Contract performance: account creation, social/match/chat features.
- Legitimate interest: service security, fraud prevention, debugging.
- Consent: where platform permissions are requested (e.g., photo library access for profile image upload).

## 4) Third-Party Processors

We use Google Firebase services:

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore
- Firebase Storage
- Cloud Functions for Firebase

These providers may process data on our behalf under their terms and data processing agreements.

## 5) Data Storage and Transfer

Data is stored in Google Firebase infrastructure. Depending on your region and Firebase configuration, data may be processed outside your country.

## 6) Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for app operation and legal obligations.

- Account and profile data: kept until account deletion.
- Match, friend, and message data: kept while account is active unless deleted earlier by feature behavior.
- Notifications: retained for operational history unless removed.

On account deletion, associated account/profile records are deleted from app-controlled collections. Some backup and technical logs may persist for a limited period per provider policies.

## 7) Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to:

- access your personal data,
- request correction,
- request deletion,
- object to or restrict processing,
- request data portability.

You can also delete your account in-app via Account settings.

## 8) Permissions

The app requests only required permissions:

- Photo library access: to select/upload a profile picture.

If you deny this permission, profile photo upload may not work, while other app features remain available.

## 9) Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures such as authenticated access controls and encrypted transport (TLS). No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 10) Children

The app is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law. Do not use the app if you are below the required age in your jurisdiction.

## 11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will publish the latest version and update the "Last updated" date.
