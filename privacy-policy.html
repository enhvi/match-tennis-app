Privacy Policy - Match

Last updated: 2026-04-26

This Privacy Policy explains how Match ("we", "us") processes personal data when you use the app.

1) Data We Process

  • Account data: email address, authentication provider, user ID.
  • Profile data: username, display name, bio, profile photo URL, selected sports.
  • Social and match data: friends, friend requests, match requests, match responses, match status.
  • Messaging data: conversation metadata and message content between friends.
  • Notification data: in-app notification entries and preferences.

2) Why We Process Data

  • Provide account, profile, social, match, and chat functionality.
  • Operate notifications and preference settings.
  • Maintain reliability, abuse prevention, and security.

3) Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase services such as Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Storage, and Cloud Functions.

4) Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed for app operation and legal obligations. You can request deletion by deleting your account in-app.

5) Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, and portability.

6) Contact

Email: Match.App@proton.me

Stand: 26.04.2026

  • Kontodaten: E-Mail-Adresse, Anmeldemethode, Benutzer-ID.

  • Bereitstellung der Konto-, Profil-, Social-, Match- und Chatfunktionen.

Wir nutzen Google Firebase, insbesondere Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Storage und Cloud Functions.

Daten werden nur so lange gespeichert, wie es fur den Betrieb und rechtliche Pflichten erforderlich ist. Eine Loschung kann uber die Kontoloschung in der App veranlasst werden.

5) Ihre Rechte

Je nach anwendbarem Recht konnen Sie Auskunft, Berichtigung, Loschung, Einschrankung, Widerspruch und Datenubertragbarkeit verlangen.
